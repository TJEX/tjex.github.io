Welcome to TJEX! ようこそ！

Welcome to TJEX! A friendly community of individuals enthusiastic about the Japanese language and culture. We provide a structured, organized language exchange between language learners and advanced/native speakers of English and Japanese.

TJEX is for individuals wishing to practice or improve their fluency in Japanese or English. All levels of language are welcome, even complete beginners. It’s also an excellent opportunity to meet new people, practice your English/Japanese, and have fun.

Our language exchange is every Saturday & Wednesday evening, with optional dinner or coffee afterwards. We also have many special events, often taking us outside of Toronto, including our annual Maple syrup sugar bush tours, Hanami, ice wine & Niagara Falls tours, St. Jacobs Farmers’ Market trip, and girls-only events.
